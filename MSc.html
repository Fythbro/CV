Modules

  • Law for Property Professionals - Assessed the impact of the Landlord and Tenant Act 1988 on the commercial property sector, and created arguments in response to specific scenarios which i assessed in terms of english law.
  • Project Implementation Process

  • Space Strategies and Legislation
